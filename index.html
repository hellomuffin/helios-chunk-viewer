Helios — Multi-prompt vs Single-prompt Long Video

Controlled ablation. Both columns are the same Helios-Base setup — autoregressive, 6 chunks of 33 frames (num_latent_frames_per_chunk=9), per-chunk VAE decode, Unified History Injection — differing in only one thing: the prompt schedule. A feeds a different step instruction per chunk (multi-prompt). B feeds one aggregated prompt to every chunk — this is the paper's default long-video mode. Comparing them isolates what changing the instruction per chunk does to continuity and to whether the task's steps actually get executed.
